# Security

### Is it safe? Where is my money stored?&#x20;

It's completely safe. Your money is always in your own wallet. Our application only helps you make transactions with them. We do not have access to your funds.

### What happens if your app disappears?&#x20;

Nothing! The funds are always in your own TON wallet. We are not the custodian of your assets; we only provide a convenient way to work with them.
