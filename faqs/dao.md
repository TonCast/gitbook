# DAO

## Benefits for DAO Participants:

Implemented:

1. You get a share of the profit: Part of the platform's revenue is distributed among everyone who has staked their TCAST. The more tokens you have staked, the greater your profit.

#### Planned for Implementation:

2. You make decisions: You vote on key changes to the project, such as reducing fees, adding new types of events (sports, politics, memes), and partnerships with other projects. You can also propose your own ideas for the platform's development.
3. You get exclusive access: DAO participants are the first to get access to new features, exclusive events, and private activities.
