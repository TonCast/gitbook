## Staking: Costs and Minimums

To ensure the security and efficiency of the staking process, the protocol has two key parameters:

1\. Minimum Staking Amount: 100 TCAST

* &#x20;This is the smallest amount of TCAST tokens you need to start earning rewards. You can stake any amount equal to or greater than 100 TCAST.

2\. Network Fee (Gas) for Staking/Unstaking: \~0.4 TON

* This is a blockchain network fee, not a platform commission. It is paid to the TON network validators for processing the transaction.
* &#x20;This fee is charged for both staking (locking your tokens) and unstaking (withdrawing them).
