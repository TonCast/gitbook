# Toncast minter contract

This smart contract is the secure and programmable foundation that governs the minting of TCAST tokens. It ensures that all tokens are issued according to the predefined and transparent rules of our tokenomics, guaranteeing fairness and predictability for the entire community.

Just like with our NFT contracts, we believe in full transparency. The source code for the TCAST Minter smart contract is open-source, allowing anyone to verify its logic and security.
