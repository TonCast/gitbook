# Create Market

### How can I create my own market?

You can create your own prediction market on TonCast. Your market will be reviewed by our team and, if approved, will be published on the platform. You will receive 20 TCAST tokens to your platform balance as a reward for creating quality content.

### What do I need to create a market?

To create a market, you need:

1. A connected TON wallet
2. A market name
3. A detailed description of the market
4. An image for your market
5. End date and time for the market in UTC+0 timezone (when the outcome will be known)

### How to fill out the market creation form?

Market Name: Enter a clear and concise name for your prediction market. This should describe what users will be predicting.

Description: Provide a detailed description of the market. Explain the conditions under which the outcome will be considered YES or NO. Be as specific as possible to avoid confusion. You must include a link to a reliable source where the outcome can be verified.

Image: Upload an image that represents your market. This helps users quickly identify your market. The image will be uploaded to our servers automatically.

End Date and Time: Select when the market will conclude and the outcome will be known. This must be a future date and time (UTC+0 timezone). After this time, no new bets can be placed.

Important: Once you submit your market, it will be sent for review. Our team will check if it meets our guidelines and approve it if everything is correct.

### What happens after I submit my market?

After submission, your market request will be reviewed by our team. You can track the status of your request in the "My Markets" section.

If approved: Your market will be published and users will be able to place bets on it. You will receive 20 TCAST tokens to your platform balance as a reward for creating quality content.

If rejected: You can view the rejection reason in the "My Markets" section. You can edit your market based on the feedback and resubmit it for review again.
