# Trade and orders

* Order Book - List of all active purchase limit orders showing current market offers.
* Coefficient - Reflects the probability of the outcome (for example, a ticket of 0.70 TON = 70% probability).
* Liquidity of tickets - Purchased tickets cannot be sold until the event is over. This rule guarantees the integrity of the market.
* Matching - The process of matching participant odds.
* TON - Blockchain on which TON Cast works. Provides speed, low commissions and integration with Telegram.
