# How to start and deposit

### How do I start? Is it difficult?

No, it's very simple and takes less than a minute:

1. Click "Connect Wallet" and connect your TON wallet. [(If you still don't have a wallet and want to create one, click here)](https://ton.org/en/wallets?locale=en\&pagination\[limit]=-1)

<p align="center"><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Снимок экрана 2025-09-24 в 17.32.10.png" alt="" data-size="original"></p>

2. Select an event.

<p align="center"><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Снимок экрана 2025-10-02 в 13.53.24 (1).png" alt=""></p>

3. Choose an outcome: Buy YES or Buy NO.
4. Select the order type: Market (at the market odds) or Limit (you specify your own odds).

<p align="center"><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Снимок экрана 2025-10-02 в 13.59.44.png" alt=""></p>

5. Make your first trade by confirming the transaction in your wallet.



### What is the commission?

1. Contract service fee per pair: 0.06 TON What is this? This is a fee for processing, secure execution, and recording your bet on the blockchain.
2. Network fee (Gas Fee): \~0.003 TON What is this? This is a fee for the initial transaction to the smart contract.
3. Platform fee: This is a small fee for using the TonCast platform itself. We only charge it if your prediction turns out to be correct. This commission is only 1%, and 99% of your winnings remain with you.
