# Tokens and rewards

* TCAST - Ton cast token. Gives the right to participate in staking.
* Platform Balance - Tokens you earned within the app through tasks, referrals, creating markets, and other activities. You can spend these tokens on Market order type bets.
* Staking - Blocking TCAST tokens to get a share of the platform's revenue.
* Reward - Income that steakrs receive from the platform.
* Tasks - Challenges that reward users with TCAST tokens for platform activity (inviting friends, matching tickets, creating markets).
* Epoch - Epoch is a fixed period of time (for example, 1 week or 1 month) during which your stacked TCAST tokens are motionless in a DAO smart contract. It is for each completed era that rewards are accrued. The longer you keep tokens in staking (the more epochs pass), the more you earn.
* NFT stakeholder- This is your DAO membership document. It is issued at the time of staking and stored in your wallet. All the key information is recorded in its metadata: Creator's address: DAO TON Cast smart contract address. Number of tokens: The amount of TCAST you have taken. Timestamp of the beginning of the era: Date and time when you started the staking period.
