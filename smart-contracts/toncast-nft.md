# Toncast nft

Beyond the TCAST token, our ecosystem is enriched with a collection of unique Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs). These are not just digital collectibles; they represent special membership, achievements, and exclusive rights within the TCAST community.

The entire logic and management for our NFT collection—including minting, transferring, and ownership tracking—are handled by smart contracts deployed on the TON blockchain. This ensures transparency, security, and true decentralization for all our digital assets.

To ensure full transparency and community ownership, the source code for our NFT smart contracts is open for anyone to view, audit, and verify.
