# Tasks and Rewards

### What are Tasks?

Tasks are challenges that reward you with TCAST tokens to your platform balance for being active on the platform. Complete tasks to earn tokens that you can use for staking or other platform features.

### Where can I find Tasks?

Go to the "Earn" section in the main menu to see all available tasks and your progress.

### What tasks are available?

**Invite Friends**

Invite users who will match at least 10 tickets on the platform. The more friends you invite, the more TCAST tokens you earn.

Task Rewards:

- 2 friends: 10 TCAST
- 5 friends: 15 TCAST
- 10 friends: 25 TCAST
- 20 friends: 50 TCAST
- 50 friends: 150 TCAST
- 100 friends: 250 TCAST

Referral Bonus: You will also receive 0.2% of the winnings from each user you invited. This is an ongoing reward that you earn every time your referrals win.

**Matched Tickets**

Place bets and match tickets with other users. The more tickets you match, the more rewards you receive.

Rewards:

- 10 matched tickets: 0.5 TCAST
- 100 matched tickets: 5 TCAST
- 1,000 matched tickets: 50 TCAST

### How do I invite friends?

In the "Earn" section, find the Invite Friends task and tap the "Share" button. This will open sharing options with your unique referral link. Share this link with friends.

### How to claim rewards?

Once you reach a milestone in any task, the "Claim Reward" button will become active. Click it to receive your TCAST tokens to your platform balance.

### Can I complete tasks multiple times?

Each task milestone can be claimed only once. However, you can progress through all milestones of each task to maximize your rewards.
